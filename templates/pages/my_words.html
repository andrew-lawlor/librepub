Kobo's 'My Words' Feature

Many of the more recent Kobo eReaders have an experimental 'My Words' feature. Although labeled experimental, I've found it to be quite stable and I've never had any problems with it.

My Words basically allows you to save words as you look them up in the dictionary while reading. This is a great way to build up your vocabulary. LibrePub allows you to take this one step further by facilitating the export of your saved words to the .tsv file format, which can be easily imported into Anki or other flashcard apps.

To enable this feature, simply:

  1. Navigate to More > Beta Features.
  2. Check the 'My Words' option.
  3. View your saved words under Activity > My Words.
